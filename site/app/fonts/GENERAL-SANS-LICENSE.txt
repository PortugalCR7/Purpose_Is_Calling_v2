General Sans — self-hosted subset (Regular 400, Medium 500 only)

Source: https://www.fontshare.com/fonts/general-sans
Publisher: Indian Type Foundry, distributed via Fontshare
License: Fontshare Free Font EULA (FF EULA), full text below.

The FF EULA explicitly permits use "in any media (including Print, Web,
Mobile, Digital, Apps, ePub, Broadcasting and OEM) at any scale, at any
location worldwide" free of charge, for personal or commercial use
(§01). Self-hosting these two static woff2 files for this site's own
use is covered by that grant. The EULA's §02 restriction on "font
serving" refers to running a font-serving/replacement service for third
parties (comparable to Cufon/EOT-style redistribution), not to a site
self-hosting the font for its own pages — that redistribution restriction
is why the files are committed here rather than fetched at runtime from
Fontshare's CDN, and why only two weights are included rather than the
full family.

Only GeneralSans-Regular.woff2 (400) and GeneralSans-Medium.woff2 (500)
are included, matching design/tokens.json -> typography.weight ("Two
weights only, regular and medium. Never bold.").
