## **My Approach.**

Just as each individual possesses unique gifts, every guide brings their own style and approach to the journey. My approach to Purpose Guiding is rooted in meeting you exactly where you are, adapting to your needs as you navigate this transformative and life-changing process**.**These pillars serve as the foundation for our work together, creating a space where your purpose can emerge and thrive.

Ready to see if we’re the right fit?**Join me for a [free 30-minute session](https://calendly.com/purposeiscalling/purpose-discovery-call?month=2024-10)**—a chance to connect, explore, and envision your journey of purpose discovery with me as your guide.

### Deep Listening

- **Tune into Your Inner Voice**: Connect deeply with your intuition, trusting it to guide you toward your Highest Calling.

- **Release Limiting Patterns**: Investigate what has held you back and gently release old beliefs that no longer serve your growth.

- **Body & Mind Alignment**: Acknowledge sensations, thoughts, and emotions, allowing your entire being to participate in this journey.

- **Create New Agreements**: Develop healthy boundaries that honor your emerging purpose and align with your true self.


### Self-Awareness & Emotional Presence

- **Embrace Your Inner Experience**: Dive into your emotions and explore what begins to surface throughout the process of discovery: ex._What is this wanting to tell me? How do I see this a place to grow and expand? What have I not been wanting to see, hear, express, recognize? Where have I been holding myself back and withholding myself from?_

- **Shift to Self-Love**: Move away from shame and judgment, embracing radical self-acceptance and love.

- **See Yourself Clearly**: Cultivate a fresh perspective on how you move in the world, rooted in clarity, confidence, and a knowing that you have so much to offer.


### Navigation Strategy

- **Set a Clear Intention**: Each journey should have a guiding vision, an intention, or sense of direction—this is your map, not a rigid itinerary. This allows us to stay away from "having to" or "needing to be" or "chasing the clock"

- **Document & Reflect:** Documenting, sharing, expressing where and what you've experinced will be a valuable asset. The Map that is created not only let's one see how far they've come, it will support going deeper with what is unearthed along the way;

- **Embrace Continuous Unfolding:** Purpose is not a destination but an ongoing journey that evolves as you take each step forward, allowing us to harness and wield our gifts for the greater good.


### Connection to Wholeness

- **Engage with the Seven Facets of Wholeness**: Connect with your inner archetypes—_They are, as illustrated by Bill Plotkin in Wild Mind and Journey of Soul Initiation, The Nurturing Generative Adult (North), The Sage/Innocent (East), The Wild Indigenous One (South), The Muse/Beloved (West), Spirit (Above), Soul (Below) and the (Mature) Ego/Human (Center);_

- **Build Relationships with Each Facet**: Strengthen your relationship with these facets as you integrate them into your being, forming a foundation for your purpose.

- **Align with Your Whole Self**: Bring together these facets to support a holistic, fully expressed version of yourself.


### Open Communication

- **Create Space for Honest Dialogue**: Engage in open, transparent communication with yourself and all parts of you, fostering understanding and integration.

- **Demystify Intuition**: Practice listening to and trusting your intuition to deepen self-awareness.

- **Safe & Authentic Expression**: Use this space to practice expressing yourself openly and honestly, without judgment or fear.


### Self-Care and Resourcing

- **Develop Supportive Practices**: Establish routines like breathwork, movement, and sound to keep you grounded and resourced during transformation.

- **Create a Loving Council**: Build a supportive network and practices to lean on when challenges arise.

- **Set Loving Boundaries**: Honor where you are and set boundaries that nurture your growth and well-being.
