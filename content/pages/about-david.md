### **About David Miranda**

###### **My Purpose is Devotion, and my Mission is to Serve others as they navigate their unique path & journey towards Wholeness-with curiosity and an adventurous, loving heart.**

##### My Story

The story of the Lone-Wolf began in my youth.

As the only child of a single mother, I was thrust into the wild terrain of emotional survival with these words: “Lock the door, don’t answer the phone, go to bed by 9 p.m, and I’ll be home later.”

In that instant, I became “independent,” “self-reliant,” and felt the stark reality of being "on my own". This was the moment I wrapped myself in armor—shielding my body, my heart, and my inner child. And in that protective cocoon…

The Lone-Wolf was born.

For years, I wore this identity like a badge of honor. My Wolf rejoiced in the freedom of independence, embracing the vast wilderness. I spent my days testing boundaries, exploring the far reaches of my world. From sunrise to sunset, I stumbled, fought, played, laughed, cried, and pushed myself to the edges. I was scared, curious, joyful, and hungry—for something more.

Through it all, I sought connection—hunting for a Pack to belong to. But when no Pack could contain me, I settled into being the Lone Wolf, howling my song into the void.

Yet hidden within these years of disconnection & emotional wandering would reveal a deep longing that prepared me to receive some of the most profound and transformative GIFTS in my life!

**1)** **Freedom to Be Unapologetically Myself:**Not fitting into a Pack freed me from the illusion of “belonging” and allowed me to see my uniqueness as a _superpower_. This revelation was a burst of energy! I didn’t need to mold myself to fit in; I was destined to break free and stand out. In embracing my individuality, I discovered the strength to blaze my own trail and call my own Soul back home, a return to my Sacred Purpose.

**2)** **Reconnecting to My True Essenc****e:**Navigating my own path led me deeper into Self-Discovery. My intuition sharpened; my connection to life became richer and more vibrant as I connected with the facets of my being - to my heart, to my body, to my Little-Boy, to my Human, to my Primate, to my Animal, to my Wild, to my Dark, to my Lovers, to my Wolf and the Soul's longing. Every day felt like a new opportunity to dive into uncharted waters and awaken parts of me that had been dormant for so long.

**3)** **Transforming Pain into Purpose:**The self-exiling, protecting my heart from the pain of disconnection and isolation was like a constant storm. But over time, I realized that I had the power to calm that storm—not by hiding, but by choosing to step out of my armor, piece by piece. This vulnerability didn’t weaken me; it forged an even greater strength within. As I faced my deepest fears, I unearthed the power of CHOICE—opening up to possibility, love, and connection.

**4)** **Becoming the Architect of My Life:** Choosing to embrace my Wholeness was a turning point. I became the architect of my life, designing a world that resonated with my Soul’s Sacred Purpose. Through the willingness to be open and create meaningful connections, I found that Wholeness wasn’t something I had to achieve—it was already within me, waiting to be seen, honored, and lived out loud.

These insights weren’t just philosophical realizations—they were life-changing _gifts_ that allowed me to bridge the gap between who I was and who I was destined to become.

Overcoming these inner obstacles, learning to lean into trust, and moving toward people wasn’t just a personal victory—it was a rebirth. I said _YES_ to relationships (with Self, Lovers, Soul, Humanity), even when it meant embracing the vulnerability and fears. Doing so freed me from stories of anger, pain, and unworthiness that had kept me caged.

I chose to _Wake Up, Grow Up, and Show Up_ fully, daring to fall in love with LIFE again after years of withholding from it.

This CHOICE—to live and love fully—fueled my ongoing commitment to Devotional Service. I flourished in ways I had only imagined, stepping boldly into my Aliveness, my Wholeness, and my Highest Calling. And it was in this flourishing that I discovered the deepest longing of all: living in alignment with my Soul’s Sacred Purpose.

This journey—from the depths of isolation to the heights of Purpose—is why I am able to Mentor, Support, and Guide others today. Because I’ve lived it, walked it, and transformed through it.

And now, I’m here to help you _Ignite your own Sacred Purpose_—to stand in your Wholeness and say YES to the life you’re meant to live.
